Welcome to CryptArch

Select a cryptocurrency and explore its price chart. Click on a data point to see related news.

CryptArch is your minimal, data-focused companion for understanding the world of crypto. With a clean interface and fast performance, you can quickly navigate between different assets and analyze their trends over time. Whether you're tracking Bitcoin, Ethereum, or lesser-known altcoins — CryptArch gives you instant access to historical pricing data and time-specific news events.

Use our responsive charts to visualize changes in value across various timeframes: 24 hours, 7 days, 1 month, or custom ranges. Each point on the graph is clickable, allowing you to view news articles published during that exact period — helping you correlate market movement with real-world events in seconds.
